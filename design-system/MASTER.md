# JRAG Design System (MASTER)

## Core Identity
- **Name**: Jaimetchoujena Rio Aqua Group (JRAG)
- **Vibe**: Scientist-Poet. Rigorous Industrial meets Noble Life.
- **Industry**: Premium Water & Seafood (Aquaculture).

## Visual Language
- **Style Pattern**: Liquid Glass + Swiss Modernism 2.0.
- **Design Philosophy**: High-contrast, structured layouts with fluid, organic motion.
- **Colors**:
  - **Rio Navy** (`#002B5C`): Primary B2B, Trust, Depth.
  - **Living Orange** (`#FF6B00`): Primary B2C, Energy, Vitality.
  - **Dakhla Sand** (`#F0EAD6`): Accent, Origin, Premium.
  - **Heritage Blue** (`#2A6DB5`): Secondary.
  - **Atmosphere Blue** (`#A4D8E5`): Accents/Highlights.

## Typography
- **Headings**: `Playfair Display` (Serif) - Heritage, Excellence.
- **Body/UI**: `Montserrat` (Sans-serif) - Modernity, Industrial Rigor.

## Components & UX
- **Buttons**:
  - B2B: Sharp corners, Rio Navy background, Dakhla Sand text.
  - B2C: Rounded corners (sm), Living Orange background, White text.
- **Cards**: Glassmorphism with subtle borders (`border-white/10`).
- **Transitions**: Smooth (300-500ms).
- **Scrolling**: Lenis (Smooth Scroll).
- **Animation**: GSAP (ScrollTrigger reveals).

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid
- **No Emojis**: Use Lucide/Heroicons SVG.
- **No Harsh Shadows**: Use soft, layered shadows or glass effects.
- **No Clutter**: Maintain high whitespace (Dakhla Sand as whitespace).
- **No Generic Icons**: Use industrial/marine-themed icons.
